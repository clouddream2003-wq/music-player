Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Music Player Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We prioritize your privacy and operate under a "no-collection" philosophy.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Offline First: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.
  • Audio Files: The App scans your device storage to index audio files. This data is used only to build your local library.
  • Media Metadata: Titles, artists, and album art are processed locally to display your music library.
  • Usage Preferences: Settings like volume, shuffle mode, and play history (Recent History) are stored locally in your App's private SharedPreferences.

2. Permissions Explained

To provide music playback features, we require the following permissions:

  • Media/Storage: Used by the MusicScanner to find and play your local audio files.
  • Foreground Service: Allows the App to continue playback and show notifications when you are using other apps.
  • Bluetooth: Used to support playback through external speakers and headphones.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. Your listening habits remain private to your device.

4. Children’s Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this local-only privacy approach, please contact us at: support@clouddream.com
