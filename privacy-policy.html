Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Thank you for using CloudDream Audio Player ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. We believe in your privacy; our app is designed to be offline-first, meaning your personal music data stays on your device.

1. Data We Collect (and Don't Collect)

1.1 Local Music Files

Our app scans your device's storage to find audio files, album art, and metadata (Artist, Title, Genre). This process happens entirely locally on your device. We do not upload your music library or listening history to any cloud server.

1.2 User Preferences

We store your preferences (such as "Sleep Timer" duration, "Sort Order," and theme settings) locally in your device's standard SharedPreferences.

1.3 Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email address, or phone number. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

2. App Permissions

To function correctly, CloudDream requires specific permissions:

  • Storage (Read/Write): Required to display your songs and allow you to rename or delete files.
  • System Settings (Write): Required only if you use the "Set as Ringtone" feature.
  • Internet Access: Required strictly for the "Support" features described below.

3. Third-Party Services & Internet Usage

While CloudDream is an offline player, we use the internet for two specific functions in the "Support" menu:

A. Currency Conversion
To display donation tiers in your local currency, we query an open-source exchange rate API (open.er-api.com). This request sends your IP address to the API provider solely to fulfill the request. No personal data is attached.

B. Payments (Donations)
If you choose to support us, we use Stripe to process payments. When you click "Support Now," you are redirected to a secure Stripe Checkout page. We never see or store your credit card details. All financial transactions are handled directly by Stripe's secure servers.

4. Changes to Files

CloudDream allows you to modify your files (Rename, Delete, Edit Tags). These actions are irreversible. We are not responsible for accidental deletion of data caused by user action.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
